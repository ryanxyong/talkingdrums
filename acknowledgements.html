Acknowledgements

This project on talking drums and how they preserve culture would not have been possible without the help of Professor Laura McPherson, Associate Professor of Linguistics at Dartmouth College, and Professor Hafiz F. Shabazz, Adjunct Associate Professor at Dartmouth College. I thank Professor McPherson for providing me with wonderful materials that she had acquried through her own research. I am grateful for all the support they have given me as I took on this endeavor to not only learn about but produce information on talking drums.
